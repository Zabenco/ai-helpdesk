RAG IT Department - Employee Offboarding Procedure
Document ID: IT-PROC-002

Purpose: Secure and standardized process for employee departures.

Phase 1: Notification & Preparation
1. HR submits Offboarding Ticket in Jira with departure date and time.
2. If termination is involuntary, HR will flag the ticket as "URGENT/IMMEDIATE" - Skip to Phase 2 immediately upon notification.

Phase 2: Access Revocation (Automated via Okta)
1. At the scheduled time, Okta lifecycle management suspends the user account.
2. Active Directory account is disabled and moved to the "Terminated Users" OU.
3. Google Workspace account password is changed, sessions revoked, and account suspended.
4. AWS IAM Identity Center access is automatically cut off.
5. All active sessions in Slack, Jira, Confluence, and Zoom are terminated.

Phase 3: Data Retention & Hardware Recovery
1. User's Google Drive ownership is transferred to their direct manager.
2. Email routing is set up: incoming emails auto-reply with a standard departure message and forward to the manager for 30 days.
3. IT sends a prepaid FedEx box to remote users for hardware return.
4. Once hardware is received, it is wiped via Jamf/Intune and placed in inventory, or sent for e-waste recycling if past lifecycle (4 years).

Checklist completion must be signed off by a Tier 2 Support Engineer.
